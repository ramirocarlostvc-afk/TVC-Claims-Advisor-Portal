Claim Document Verification Guide

Select your primary objective below to load details of the mandatory documents and official VA forms required by accredited claims officers to establish benefits under 38 CFR.

Disability Compensation

  • Intent to File (VA Form 21-0966) - Establishes retroactive claim date.
  • VA Form 21-22 - Appoints TVC as your Accredited Power of Attorney.
  • DD-214 Certificate - Must show discharge character (Honorable, Under Honorable, etc.).
  • Service Medical Records (STRs) - Showing in-service event or diagnosis.
  • VA Form 21-526EZ - The actual application for Compensation.

Low-Income Pension

  • Intent to File (VA Form 21-0966) - Preserves retroactive pension date.
  • VA Form 21P-527EZ - Direct application for veterans disability pension.
  • Wartime Service Validation - Shows 90+ days active duty with 1 day in recognized war.
  • Net Worth & Income Records - Under standard limits for financial need.
  • Permanently Disabled Diagnosis - If under age 65, medical verification required.

Survivor & Dependents

  • VA Form 21P-534EZ - Application for DIC, Death Pension, and Accrued Benefits.
  • Veteran's Death Certificate - Certifying cause of death or service rating status.
  • Marriage Certificate / Birth Cert - Validating dependent child or spouse status.
  • VA Form 21-686c - Declaration of Status of Dependents.

Key VA Forms Index

Form # Official Title Purpose & Importance
Form 21-0966 Intent to File Saves retroactive effective date for up to 364 days before formal submission.
Form 21-526EZ Application for Disability Compensation Primary form used for original, new, secondary, or increased claims.
Form 21-22 Appointment of VSO Formally designates TVC as your legal representative before the VA.
Form 21P-527EZ Application for Pension Claim form for war-time veterans meeting specific physical and income thresholds.
Form 20-0995 Supplemental Claim Application Filing for review under AMA when new and relevant evidence is discovered.
Form 20-0996 Higher-Level Review (HLR) Request for a senior rater review of existing evidence with zero new documents allowed.

Title 38 CFR Index

38 CFR Part 3 (Adjudication) Critical

Defines rules for compensation, pension, death benefits, and general criteria of service connection.

38 CFR Part 4 (Schedule for Ratings)

Houses the actual tables and rating diagnostic codes used to evaluate specific diseases and injuries.

38 CFR Part 20 (Board Appeals)

Establishes guidelines and procedures for appeals submitted to the Board of Veterans' Appeals (BVA).
